About Rakshita

Care Instructions

Keep your embroidered handkerchiefs looking beautiful with these simple care tips.

💧

Hand Wash Gently

Use cold water and mild detergent. Avoid harsh scrubbing to protect the embroidery.

🌡️

Use Mild Detergent

Choose gentle, color-safe detergents. Avoid bleach or harsh chemicals.

🌬️

Air Dry Away from Sun

Lay flat or hang in shade to dry. Direct sunlight can fade colors over time.

🔄

Iron on Reverse Side

If ironing is needed, use low heat on the back side to protect embroidery.

📦

Store Flat or Rolled

Keep in a drawer or roll gently to prevent creasing and maintain shape.

❌

Avoid Harsh Chemicals

No bleach, fabric softeners, or dry cleaning chemicals on embroidered areas.
